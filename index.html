ABOUT DREJ

As a software developer, I have a passion for creating efficient and user-friendly applications that solve complex problems. My journey into the world of software development began when working in the sneaker retail industry. I saw it was becoming increasingly hard to obtain the latest and hottest sneakers, so I wanted to learn the skills necessary to create applications that would give consumers an even playing field when trying to get the new hyped releases.

With a background in the retail industry, my passion for coding grew even stronger while working on a project that required intense focus and problem-solving ability. This experience opened my eyes to the power of software and inspired me to pursue a career in this field.

Since then, I have honed my skills in various programming languages and technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Tailwind. I have worked on projects ranging from e-commerce to social media and task tracker applications, and I'm always excited to take on new challenges and explore new technologies.

My goal as a software developer is to create innovative solutions that make a difference in people's lives. Whether it's improving business processes or enhancing user experiences, I believe that software has the power to transform the world, and I'm proud to be a part of this exciting field.
